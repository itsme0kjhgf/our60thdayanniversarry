Our Story

It all started one day when I was on a transport scolaire with my friends. I looked up into the mirror and there she was – the most beautiful girl I had ever seen in my life. It felt like a dream. From that moment, I couldn’t stop thinking about her. The next year, we ended up in the same class, and I was beyond happy.

I was shy at first, but she made the first move by asking for my Instagram, and from there, we started chatting every Friday and Saturday night. Those were the best nights – we'd always say goodnight to each other, and I couldn't wait for the next conversation.

Before we started dating, I was afraid to tell her how I felt, but with the encouragement of our friend Rafiq, I finally found the courage. On December 3rd, I told her my true feelings. To my relief, she had felt the same way all along. She had been afraid of losing such an amazing friendship by sharing her feelings.

From that day on, I became the happiest person alive. Every day with her feels like a dream, and I can’t wait to see what the future holds for us.
